Computer Scientist

Who am I?

I'm a computer science PhD candidate at Florida International University, working at the Bioinformatics Research Group (BioRG). I work on modelling heterogeneous microbial interactions under a Dynamic Bayesian framework from temporally misaligned longitudinal data, Data Mining application on the microbiome of Bacterial Vaginosis, and study of dimensionality reduction algorithms like PLS-DA or PCA. Currently on my fourth year, I have achieved the maximum score in all of the courses and have published several papers in important journals and conferences.
